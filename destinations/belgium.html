Brussels & Antwerp, Belgium

Fall, 2026

A textile and fashion-focused adventure in Brussels and Antwerp with private tours of fashion museums, studio visits with textile artists, private shopping experiences, a tour of Belgium’s leading fashion academy, exclusive hotels and sought-after restaurants.

Highlights:

• Private tours in Brussels of the Fashion Museum Antwerp (main exhibition and special access to the archive), the Fashion & Lace Museum and the Fashion and Textile Collection. at the Royal Museum of Art and History

• Special visits to the home of renowned antiques dealer and interior designer Axel Vervoordt, and architect and Belgian Designer of the Year, Vincent Van Duysen

• Private tour of Zaventem Ateliers which brings together 21 creators, designers and artists in a former industrial space united by their passion for materials.

• A visit to the Fashion Department at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts, Antwerp (former students include leading designers Ann Demeulemeester, Walter Van Beirendonck, Dries van Noten, Martin Margiela)

• Studio visits with textile artists Alice Leens, Christoph Hefti, Geneviève Levivier.

• Accommodation at luxury 5-star hotels such as Hotel Botanic Sanctuary Antwerp and Steigenberger Wiltcher’s in Brussels.

• Meals at the most sought-after restaurants in Brussels and Antwerp including Senzanome, La Villa in the Sky, Hof van Cleve, Zilte, Bourry.

Upon confirmation of the trip, Liaisons will carry out in-depth research on the destination based on the interests of the institution.
